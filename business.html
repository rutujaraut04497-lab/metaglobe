Course Syllabus

Month 1: Foundations of Business Analysis

Week 1: Introduction to Business Analysis
  • What is Business Analysis?
  • Role of a Business Analyst
  • BA in SDLC & Agile Lifecycle
  • Stakeholders & their expectations
  • Key deliverables of a BA
Week 2: SDLC, Methodologies & Project Environment
  • SDLC: Waterfall vs Agile vs Hybrid
  • Agile: Scrum Framework (Roles, Artifacts, Ceremonies)
  • Business Process Management
  • Project Ecosystem: Developers, QA, PM, UI/UX, Clients
Week 3: Requirement Engineering
  • Requirement Gathering Techniques (Interviews, Surveys, Workshops)
  • Elicitation & Brainstorming
  • Functional vs Non-functional requirements
  • Prioritization Techniques: MoSCoW, Kano Model
Week 4: Documentation & BA Artefacts
  • Business Requirement Document (BRD)
  • Functional Requirement Specification (FRS)
  • Software Requirement Specification (SRS)
  • Use Case Documentation
  • Change Request Documents

Week 5: Tools for BAs
  • Microsoft Excel for BA tasks
  • PowerPoint for Presentations
  • MS Visio for Process Flows

  • Introduction to SQL basics (Read-only)

  • UML Diagrams: Use Case, Activity, Sequence, Class

  • Writing good User Stories (INVEST Model)
